# Price Changes

Rules for when the founder modifies plan prices, overage, or extra seats.

**Principle:** Base price charged in advance. Customer already paid → changes apply at renewal.

## Plan Base Price

| Change | New Customers | Existing Customers |
|--------|---------------|---------------------|
| Increase price | Immediate | At renewal |
| Decrease price | Immediate | At renewal |

## Overage Price (Usage-Based)

| Change | Behavior |
|--------|----------|
| Increase/Decrease | At renewal |

Price in effect at **start of period** is used for that period's billing.

## Extra Seat Price

Same as overage: changes apply at renewal.

## Multiple Changes in One Period

Each customer pays what was in effect at start of THEIR period. Doesn't matter how many times it changes.

```
Day 1: Price = $99
Day 10: Founder increases to $119
Day 20: Founder decreases to $109

Customer A (period 1-30): Pays $99
Customer B (subscribed day 15): Pays $119
Customer C (subscribed day 25): Pays $109
```
